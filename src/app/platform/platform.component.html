Platform

What is Polarity?

po•lar•i•ty: the asymmetric organization of cellular elements, which allows development of specialized tissue and downstream function.

The PolarityTE Platform

Our platform technology harnesses natural regeneration and allows cells to function as they normally do in the body. Through the revolutionary platform, we seek to induce cells and tissue polarity, and create functional tissue. Our goal is to apply the platform across all cells, tissues and composition structures, transforming regenerative medicine into what has been envisioned since its inception.

How It Works?

Our manufacturing process is simple and flexible. Our platform allows us to develop products that are autologous—from the patient, for the patient—and homologous—skin for skin, bone for bone, etc.

Simplicity

After a small full-thickness tissue sample from a patient is sent to PolarityTE, the construct will be created and returned for application on or in the same patient.

Flexibility

Constructs are being developed to accommodate different sizes and needs across multiple tissue types. Our manufacturing process is also being developed to allow for cryopreservation to meet future patient needs.
