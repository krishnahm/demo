What is the RTA?

The advanced Real-Time Assistant (RTA) allows medical providers to communicate with the PolarityTE Physician-led Clinical Operations Team 24-hours a Day via Text, Email, Video Chat or Phone. From this HIPAA-compliant messaging platform providers can track issues, conduct peer-to-peer QA, submit images or videos for review.
