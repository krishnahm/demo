Exceptional Release Form

Exceptional Release of PolarityTE™ Human Autologous SkinTE™

Exceptional release is the dispensing of tissue because of emergent or unusual clinical need that circumvents normal release procedures, and requires release by the manufacturer's QA and by the end-user provider. A PolarityTE Specialist has reviewed the processing records available and has approved this tissue for exceptional release.

* If you have any questions, please read further on Exceptional Release using the RTA Application or call a PolarityTE Specialist at: 1-800-743-2302.
