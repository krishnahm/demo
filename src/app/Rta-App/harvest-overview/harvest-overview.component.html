This guide is not a substitute for the SkinTE™ Instructions for Use. Providers must carefully review the complete SkinTE Instructions for Use prior to harvest.

Open the All-In-One Harvest Box and Ensure All Contents are Intact

Obtain Full-Thickness Skin Graft with Fat

Place Specimen in Sterile Cup, Add Crystalloid Solution and Add Antibiotic

Place Sterile Cup (with Absorbent Pad) in Dual Bag System, Remove Excess Air and Seal

Place Specimen, Activate the NanoCool® Lid and Start the Temperature Tracking System

Place NanoCool® lid, Complete the Patient/Defect Information and Add Patient Sticker as Indicated.
